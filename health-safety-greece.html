Health & Emergency Information 🇬🇷

Important medical and emergency guidance for students travelling to Greece.

🚨 Emergency Number

Dial 112 for all emergencies (police, fire, ambulance).

🏥 Medical Treatment

Ensure you have travel insurance. European Health Insurance Cards (EHIC/GHIC) may not cover everything.

💊 Medication

Bring sufficient prescribed medication. Some medicines may require documentation.

🌡 Heat & Sun Safety

Greece can experience extreme temperatures. Stay hydrated and avoid peak sun hours.
