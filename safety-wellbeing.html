Driving in Greece 🇬🇷

Important road laws and safety guidance for students.

🚨 DO NOT Drink and Drive

Greece has lower drink-driving limits than the UK. Police conduct roadside checks regularly.

Speed Limits

50km/h in towns unless otherwise stated.

Helmets Required

Helmets are compulsory for motorcycles, scooters, and quad bikes.

Quad Bikes

Often classified as extreme sport. Insurance may not cover accidents.

Insurance

Ensure your vehicle hire includes proper insurance coverage.
